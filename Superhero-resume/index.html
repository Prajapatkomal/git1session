BIOGRAPHY

Captain Marvel

Former Air Force pilot and intelligence agent Carol Danvers pursued her dream of space exploration as a NASA employee, but her life forever changed when she was accidentally transformed into a human-Kree hybrid with extraordinary powers.

SUMMARY

Captain Marvel's early years were marked by her being overshadowed by stronger characters and her lack of involvement with the Avengers. Throughout her journey, Captain Marvel formed a significant bond with Mar-Vell, who played a crucial role in her development as a superhero. Captain Marvel achieved her full potential as a god-tier warrior when she unlocked her Binary form, showcasing her incredible strength and power.
